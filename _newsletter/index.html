What have we been up to?

When I joined Alpha Omega Epsilon I was told, "our chapter is so great because you can really get what you want out of it," and that still holds true today. One of the best things about our sisterhood is that we can easily tailor it to the current actives and their interests. Every semester we choose to participate in different philanthroipies, service events, professional events, sisterhood events, and more. No two semesters are ever the same!
