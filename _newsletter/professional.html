Professional Events

Our 2016-2017 Professional chair, Cassie Turberville, has done an excellent job setting up professional events to allow our sisters to constantly develop their professional skills. This semester our sister have developed through:

  • Study hours

    For over 25 hours a week, the sorority has private rooms reserved on campus where sisters can go to study. This year we have implemented tutoring sessions as a part of study hours where sisters can volunteer their time and knowledge to help other sisters!

  • Company info sessions

    So much is happening around campus, and Cassie makes sure sisters know what companies are in town. This is especially beneficial since not all info sessions get sent out to every department.

  • Etiquette dinner

    This semester it was held at Custom Catering.

  • Night with the Industries

    This networking event is the night before expo. Many expo company representatives are in attendance. We cosponsored this event with SWE.

  • Professional dress chapter

    Yes, it's still a thing!

  • Co-op/intern panels with sisters

    This semester we co-hosted a panel with SWE!
