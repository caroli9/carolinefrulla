About Me

I am a professional and technical writer with a digital mind. I have experience with technical documentation and science writing as well as with marketing strategies. I believe that the design of how a message is presented is just as important as the content of that message. I also believe that writing should be visually interactive regardless of whether it is online or in print, and that readers should experience it in a beautiful, simple, and visually fun way.

I am currently pursuing a B.A. in Professional and Technical Writing and a B.A. in English Literature and Language, and have a background in computer science. My experience and education has allowed me to develop a passion for helping organizations spread their values and messages in and through their content.

Feel free to download my resume, check out my LinkedIn profile and browse through my portfolio to get a feel for my technical writing and marketing experience. Contact me if you would like writing samples or to work together!

    Science Writing

    Science Writing is a class for non-scientist to learn about specialized science work. Over the semester, we studied how to research, evaluate, and synthesize peer-reviewed and specialized sources and clearly communicate it to an interested public. We were offered authentic writing experiences that demonstrated the settings, genre ecologies, conventions, limitations, and possibilities of science writing as both an means of communication and a job that real people do each day. One of our projects for class involved interviewing a graduate student working in one of the Virginia Tech labs. From this assignment, I learned how to communicate with a specialist and effectively draw out the important and translatable aspects of his research. At work, I was able to use these same skills to communicate with SMEs and develop content that I had no previous knowledge on.
